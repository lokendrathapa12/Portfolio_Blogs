About Me

Welcome to the About section of Lokendra Thapa's website.

Mr. Lokendra Thapa is a highly driven and accomplished individual hailing from the beautiful district of Jajarkot, Nepal. He completed his School Leaving Certificate (SLC) from Bal Higher Secondary School in Jajarkot in the year 2072 BS, and went on to pursue higher education in the Science faculty at GVN Secondary School in Nepalgunj. He successfully completed his +2 studies in 2074 BS, and later earned a Bachelor's degree in BSc CSIT from Nepalgunj Campus in the year 2078 BS.

The unwavering support of his parents, Bimal Thapa and Kalika Thapa, has been instrumental in Mr. Thapa's academic success. He currently resides in the vibrant town of Kirtipur, Kathmandu, where he continues to pursue his passion for learning and growth.

When he's not busy with his professional pursuits, Mr. Thapa loves to travel and explore new places, write code, and indulge in his love for movies and web series. His insatiable curiosity and thirst for knowledge have always propelled him to seek out new challenges and opportunities to expand his skill set. With a strong academic background and a diverse range of interests and experiences, Mr. Thapa is committed to making a positive impact in the world. He looks forward to collaborating with like-minded individuals on exciting new projects and initiatives. Thank you for taking the time to learn more about Mr. Lokendra Thapa.

Work Process

Research and Plan

The process of research and planning in a web development project involves identifying project goals, researching the market and competitors, creating a plan with a timeline and budget, considering SEO, accessibility, and security, and designing with user experience in mind. This process ensures that the final product meets stakeholder expectations and is functional and user-friendly.

Design and Develop

The process of designing and developing a web development project typically involves several stages, including creating wireframes and mockups, developing the front-end and back-end code, and integrating any necessary third-party services or APIs. The design and development process may also involve testing and debugging to ensure that the final product is functional and user-friendly. .

Deliver

Project delivery involves a series of processes that are typically carried out in a sequential manner. These processes may include planning, initiating, executing, monitoring, controlling, and closing. The planning process involves defining project scope, identifying deliverables, setting timelines, and creating a project plan. The initiation process involves assembling a project team and defining roles and responsibilities.
